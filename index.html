Welcome

I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at Virginia Tech. I grew up in Winchester, VA and I graduated from the University of Virignia in 2020 with a double major in Computer Science and History. My current research is in Computer Science Education and I am adivsed by Dr. Clifford Shaffer.

I have had several internships, with The Library Corporation and the McKesson Corporation, where I worked on cybersecurity and front-end and API development.

This past year at Virginia Tech I have been involved with CS Graduate Council where I have helped to organize events and attended meetings. Next year I will be serving as the Vice President to continue my work.

Learn more about my research interests and check out my CV. Reach out if you want to discuss my research, software engineering, crosswords, soccer, board games, books, and more!
